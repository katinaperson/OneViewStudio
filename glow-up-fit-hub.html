📅 Months 1–2 · Foundation Phase
📋 How This Works
🔥
Calorie Deficit = Weight Loss You'll burn ~2,000 cal/day and eat ~1,400, creating a 600 cal daily deficit — about 1.5–2 lbs/week lost.
📈
Phases Get Harder Months 1–2 build the base. Months 3–4 add intensity. Months 5–6 push for the finish line.
🏠
100% Home — No Gym Needed Every workout uses the equipment you already own. Zero excuses, zero commute.
Monday Walking Pad + Lower Body
🔥 Warm-Up (5 min)
Walking Pad · slow pace, 2.5–3 mph · 5 min to wake up the legs
🚶 Cardio (20 min)
Walking Pad · increase to 3–3.5 mph · steady, consistent pace · aim for light sweat
🏋️ Maxi Squat Circuit (15 min)
Maxi Squat — wide stance · 3 sets × 15 reps · slow and controlled
Maxi Squat — narrow stance · 3 sets × 12 reps · targets quads more
Bodyweight glute bridges · 3 sets × 20 reps · on floor, squeeze at top
Reverse lunges · 3 sets × 10 each leg · no equipment needed
🧘 Cool-Down (5 min)
Standing quad + calf stretch
Hip flexor stretch · hold 30 sec each side
Tuesday Rower + Upper Body
🚣 Sunny Rower Cardio (20 min)
Row at moderate resistance · 5 min easy warm-up pace
Row intervals · 1 min fast / 1 min easy · repeat 7 rounds
Row cool-down · 3 min easy to finish
💪 Upper Body (15 min)
Hyperbell bicep curls · 3 sets × 12 · solo bar for now, add 3 lb bells in Month 3
5 lb overhead shoulder press · 3 sets × 12 · press up, lower slowly
Hyperbell tricep overhead extension · 3 sets × 12 · hold bar overhead, lower behind head
5 lb lateral raises · 3 sets × 12 · arms out to sides, slow and controlled
Push-ups (modified on knees ok) · 3 sets × 10
🧘 Core Finisher (5 min)
Plank hold · 3 × 30 sec (build to 60 sec by Month 2)
Bicycle crunches · 2 sets × 20
Wednesday Active Rest

Light movement only — let your muscles rebuild. Choose one:

Walking Pad · 20–25 min at very easy pace (2–2.5 mph) — conversational pace
Gentle stretching · 15–20 min full body · YouTube: Yoga with Adriene
Light housework or errands · counts! Keep moving, don't sit all day
Thursday MaxiClimber HIIT
🧗 MaxiClimber XL — HIIT Session

The climber hits legs, glutes, arms, and core all at once — it's a calorie furnace. Start where you are. The progression below is your roadmap.

📅 Month 1 — Start Here
Warm-up · step on, easy pace · 2 min
Climb hard · 30 sec on · then step off
Rest · 60 sec standing rest
Repeat · 6 rounds total · that's it · honor it
Cool-down · easy pace · 2 min
📅 Month 2 — Getting Stronger
Climb hard · 30 sec on
Rest · 45 sec · (shorter rest = harder)
Repeat · 8 rounds total
📅 Month 3 — Now We're Talking
Climb hard · 45 sec on · (you'll laugh at when 30 sec wrecked you)
Rest · 45 sec
Repeat · 8 rounds total
📅 Month 4 — Building Endurance
Climb hard · 45 sec on
Rest · 30 sec · (short rest = serious calorie burn)
Repeat · 10 rounds total
📅 Months 5–6 — Final Form 🔥
Climb hard · 60 sec on · full minute beast mode
Rest · 30 sec only
Repeat · 10 rounds · this is where the last pounds come off
🏋️ Maxi Squat Add-On (10 min)
Maxi Squat — pulse squats · 3 sets × 20 pulses at the bottom · burns!
Maxi Squat — slow tempo · 3 sets × 10 · 3 sec down, 1 sec up
Friday Full Body Strength
💪 Hyperbell + Weights Circuit (35 min)

Rest 60 sec between sets. This is your strongest day of the week.

Hyperbell squat to press · 3 × 12 · squat down, press bar overhead as you stand
Hyperbell Romanian deadlift · 3 × 12 · hinge at hips, bar runs down legs
5 lb bent-over rows · 3 × 12 each arm · back flat, elbow drives to ceiling
Maxi Squat — weighted feel · 3 × 15 · use machine for extra resistance
Hyperbell Russian twists · 3 × 20 · seated, hold bar, rotate side to side
Superman hold · 3 × 15 · on floor, arms and legs lift · hold 2 sec
Saturday Long Cardio — Your Choice

Pick your machine — 40–45 min total. This is your longest session of the week. Put on a podcast or playlist and go.

Option A: Walking Pad · 40 min at 3–3.5 mph · steady state fat burn zone
Option B: Sunny Rower · 40 min moderate row · great for upper body endurance
Option C: MaxiClimber · 30 min steady climb + 10 min walking pad cool-down
Finish: 10 min full-body stretch · hips, hamstrings, chest, shoulders
Sunday Full Rest

True rest day. Let your muscles recover and rebuild. Use this day for meal prep, hydration, and rest. Honor your Sabbath. The body rebuilds on rest days — they're not wasted days, they're growth days.

📈 Equipment Progression by Phase
1️⃣
Months 1–2: Build the BaseWalking Pad, Rower, MaxiClimber at moderate intensity. Hyperbell solo (no added weight). 5 lb weights for upper body. Maxi Squat bodyweight only.
2️⃣
Months 3–4: Add ResistanceAttach 3 lb bells to Hyperbell. Increase walking pad speed to 3.5–4 mph. MaxiClimber HIIT gets longer (35 min). Row intervals go harder.
3️⃣
Months 5–6: Final PushHyperbell with 3 lb bells + controlled tempo. 2 HIIT days (Thu + one more). Long Saturday cardio extends to 50–55 min. You're almost there.

Built around YOUR foods. ~1,400 calories/day · high protein · anti-inflammatory.

Option B: Power Oatmeal ½ cup rolled oats cooked in water · 1 turkey sausage link crumbled in · ½ banana sliced on top · pinch of cinnamon · no sugar added
Option C: Quick Egg Muffins Prep Sunday: 2 eggs whisked with spinach + diced turkey sausage baked in muffin tins · grab 2 each morning · side of banana

Option A: Tuna Power Bowl 1 can tuna (in water, drained) · ½ cup cooked brown rice or 1 whole wheat pita · steamed broccoli · drizzle of olive oil + lemon · carrots on the side
Option B: Salmon Plate 4–5 oz baked salmon (season with garlic, lemon, herbs) · 1 cup steamed mixed vegetables · ½ cup zucchini sautéed with olive oil spray
Option C: Chicken Breast Wrap 4 oz grilled chicken breast sliced · whole wheat wrap or 2 slices whole wheat bread · spinach, shredded carrots, mustard (not mayo) · small bunch grapes on the side

Veggies + Dip Carrot sticks + 2 tbsp hummus · or celery + tuna on whole wheat crackers (5–6 crackers)
Smoothie as Snack Your frozen strawberry/blueberry blend (see Smoothies tab) · counts as snack, not a meal add-on
🥤 The Soda Swap Strategy

Don't go cold turkey — swap progressively. Every soda you skip saves 150 cal.

Regular soda → Sparkling water + lemon
Afternoon soda habit → Iced green tea (unsweetened)
Sugary drinks → Infused water (cucumber + mint)

Goal: Cut to 1 soda max per week by Month 2. Zero by Month 3.

Smoothie Recipes

Using your frozen fruit + fresh picks. All under 250 calories. Great as breakfast or snack.

⚡ Base Formula

Frozen fruit (1 cup) + leafy green (1 handful spinach — you won't taste it) + liquid (unsweetened almond milk or water, 1 cup) + optional protein (Greek yogurt, ½ cup) = balanced, filling smoothie.

🍓 The Berry Bliss
½ cup frozen strawberries · ½ cup frozen blueberries · 1 handful fresh spinach · 1 cup unsweetened almond milk · ½ banana · blend until smooth
~210 cal · 6g protein
🌴 Tropical Power
1 cup frozen tropical fruit mix · 1 handful spinach · ¾ cup water · ½ cup plain Greek yogurt (adds protein!) · squeeze of lime
~220 cal · 12g protein
🍌 Banana Greens
1 ripe banana (fresh or frozen) · 1 cup fresh spinach · 1 tbsp natural peanut butter · 1 cup unsweetened almond milk · pinch of cinnamon
~230 cal · 7g protein
💙 Blueberry Protein Blast
1 cup frozen blueberries · ½ cup plain Greek yogurt · ½ cup spinach · ½ cup water + ½ cup almond milk · ½ banana
~240 cal · 13g protein
