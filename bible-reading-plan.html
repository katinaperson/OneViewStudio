📖 Study Tips
✦Keep your Proverbs habit. Stack it on top of any plan — read the proverb for the day's date first, then your assigned reading.
✦Pray before you open. Even 30 seconds asking God to speak changes the experience completely.
✦Write one thing. Journal one verse, question, or observation per reading. Retention skyrockets.
✦Don't guilt-skip. If you miss a day, just pick up where you left off. The goal is a lifestyle, not a streak.
✦Pair translations. With multiple Bibles incoming, read a passage in two — one familiar, one fresh. The differences illuminate meaning beautifully.
